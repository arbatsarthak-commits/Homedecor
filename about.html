About Jiyu's HomeDecor

We are passionate about bringing the beauty and craftsmanship of traditional Indian home decor to modern living spaces, creating homes that tell stories of heritage and elegance.

Our Story

Founded in 2020, Jiyu's HomeDecor began as a small family business with a simple mission: to preserve and share the rich heritage of Indian craftsmanship with the world. Our name "Jiyu" means "to live" in Sanskrit, reflecting our belief that beautiful, thoughtfully crafted spaces enhance the way we live.

What started as a passion project has grown into a trusted brand that connects skilled artisans from across India with discerning customers who appreciate authentic, handcrafted home decor. Each piece in our collection tells a story of tradition, skill, and timeless beauty.

We work directly with over 200 artisans from 15 different states across India, ensuring fair wages and sustainable practices. Our commitment to quality and authenticity has made us a favorite among interior designers, homeowners, and collectors worldwide.
