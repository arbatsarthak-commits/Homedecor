Frequently Asked Questions

What is your return policy? +

We offer a 30-day hassle-free return policy. If you're not completely satisfied with your purchase, you can return it within 30 days of delivery for a full refund or exchange. Items must be in original condition with tags attached.

Do you offer free shipping? +

Yes! We offer free shipping across India on all orders above ₹2,000. For orders below ₹2,000, we charge a flat shipping fee of ₹150. International shipping is available with additional charges.

How long does delivery take? +

For domestic orders, delivery typically takes 3-7 business days depending on your location. We process orders within 1-2 business days and provide tracking information once your order ships.

Are your products authentic? +

Absolutely! All our products are 100% authentic and handcrafted by skilled artisans across India. We work directly with artisans and provide certificates of authenticity with each purchase.

Do you offer custom orders? +

Yes, we love creating custom pieces! Contact us with your requirements and we'll work with our artisans to create something unique for you. Custom orders typically take 2-4 weeks to complete.
